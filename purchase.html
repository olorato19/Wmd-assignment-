How to Buy

  1. Browse Listings: Look through the products listed on our website using the categories or search bar.
  2. Select a Product: Click on a product to view more information such as price, description, and seller contact details.
  3. Contact the Seller: Use the provided contact method (phone number, email, or WhatsApp) to reach out to the seller. You can ask questions, negotiate the price, and discuss delivery or pickup options.
  4. Make Payment and Receive the Product: Arrange payment directly with the seller. Make sure to confirm all details and inspect the product before making any payment.

Important: We are only an advertisement platform and are not involved in any transactions. Always verify the seller and product before making any payments.
