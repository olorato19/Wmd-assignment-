How To Purchase

  1. Explore Cartalogue: All product details, including price and description, are shown directly on the card.
  2. Contact the Seller: Use the provided contact method (phone number, email, or WhatsApp) to reach out to the seller. You can ask questions, negotiate the price, and discuss delivery or pickup options.
  3. Make Payment and Receive the Product: Arrange payment directly with the seller. Make sure to confirm all details and inspect the product before making any payment.

Note: We only advertise products. Transactions are made directly between you and the seller. Please verify the item and seller before making any payment.
