About Gaff Inc

Gaff Inc was established in the year 2021 but Gaff is not just a brand — it’s a movement driven by ambition, edge, and creativity. We design with purpose, crafting standout pieces that blur the line between streetwear and high fashion. Every collection is a tribute to hustle culture, individuality, and those who dare to dream big. From bold graphics to refined cuts, our apparel is made for leaders, creators, and rebels. If you’re building your empire, Gaff Inc is what you wear on the way up. Rise in style. Rule in Gaff.

Misson

We're not here to follow trends — we create our own. Gaff Inc is for the ones who wear their story on their sleeves and live unapologetically. From the fabric to the fit, we put heart into every stitch so you can wear your truth, loud and proud.

Our Vision

Our vision is to grow Gaff Inc. into Botswana’s leading fashion brand, known for redefining urban and streetwear with a local identity. We aim to celebrate our culture, creativity, and everyday experiences through clothing that speaks to the youth of Botswana and Southern Africa. By staying true to our roots and community, we hope to inspire pride, confidence, and self-expression—one outfit at a time.
